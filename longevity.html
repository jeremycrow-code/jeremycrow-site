Health and Longevity for Occultists

Summary

This course explores the importance of physical health and longevity in the context of Luciferian Alchemy, challenging the demonization of the body in spiritual traditions. It emphasizes practical strategies like exercise, nutrition, and supplementation to enhance vitality and support spiritual goals, such as apotheosis, by maintaining a strong physical vessel for transformative work.

Video

Article

In many spiritual traditions, particularly those influenced by monotheistic or New Age philosophies, the body is often devalued as a "lower self" or a mere vessel for a spiritual essence. This course challenges that perspective, advocating for the body as a vital tool for occultists, especially those on the Left-Hand Path pursuing Luciferian Alchemy. Health and longevity are not just practical concerns but integral to achieving goals like apotheosis or establishing a lasting legacy.

Historically, alchemists sought the Philosopher’s Stone or elixir of life, symbols of incorruptibility and immortality, akin to transforming lead into gold. In modern terms, this translates to leveraging medical science and lifestyle choices to extend a healthy, effective life. A strong body enhances our ability to impact the material world, which may be the only plane where our actions have lasting effects during our lifetime. For a deeper exploration of alchemical principles, consider The Alchemist’s Handbook by Frater Albertus, which connects traditional alchemy to personal transformation.

Practical steps for health include regular exercise—combining cardio and weight training to maintain muscle mass and bone density, which become critical with age. Nutrition is equally vital, focusing on foods rich in commonly deficient nutrients like vitamin K2 (found in brie cheese, liver pate, or natto), which prevents organ calcification and supports bone health. Supplementation can help, such as a balanced stack of calcium, magnesium, vitamin K2, and vitamin D3, taken with meals for optimal absorption. For an introduction to establishing a healthy lifestyle aimed at complimenting an esoteric practice, download the free PDF Longevity Foundations by Jeremy Crow. For a comprehensive guide on nutrition and health, How Not to Die by Michael Greger offers evidence-based dietary advice for longevity.

Beyond physical health, brain health is crucial for maintaining cognitive faculties. Regular exercise, learning a new language, or playing a musical instrument can enhance brain complexity and delay neurodegenerative diseases like Alzheimer’s. Supplements like creatine monohydrate (for neuroprotection and muscle recovery) and lion’s mane mushroom (for nerve growth and clearing beta-amyloid plaques) support cognitive health. Chaga mushroom, a potent antioxidant, further boosts immunity and hormonal balance, making it a versatile addition to a daily routine, whether in coffee or savory dishes.

This course underscores that health and longevity are not separate from spiritual practice but foundational to it. By nurturing the body, occultists can extend their time to pursue enlightenment and transformation, embodying the Luciferian principle of empowered selfhood. Reflect on how you can integrate these practices to support your alchemical journey.
